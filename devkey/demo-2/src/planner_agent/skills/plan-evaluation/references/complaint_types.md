# Las Vegas 311 Complaint Types

## Complaint Categories

| Type | Description | Marathon Relevance |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| noise | Noise complaints (construction, events, traffic) | High — PA systems, crowds, helicopters |
| parking | Parking violations and disputes | Medium — event parking displacement |
| road_closure | Road closure and traffic disruption | High — marathon route closures |
| trash | Illegal dumping and sanitation issues | Medium — post-event cleanup |
| safety | Public safety concerns | High — crowd management, emergency access |
| pedestrian | Pedestrian access and sidewalk issues | High — spectator and resident crossings |

## Sensitivity Rating Formula

- **critical**: High residential density AND median age > 35
- **high**: High residential density
- **medium**: Medium residential density
- **low**: Low residential density
