# Revenue Model Guide

## Revenue Sources

### Registration Fees (Primary Source, 50-65% of total)
- **Early Bird**: 20% discount, first 30% of registrations
- **Standard**: Base price, 50% of registrations
- **VIP**: 2x base price, 5-10% of registrations, includes extras
- **Charity entries**: Reduced fee but guaranteed fundraising minimum

### Sponsorship Revenue (15-30% of total)
- Scales with participant count AND event maturity
- Inaugural events capture ~30% of potential
- Established events (5+ years) capture 100% of potential
- Formula: `base = participants × $15`, then apply maturity factor

### Merchandise (3-5% of total)
- Official race gear: 12% purchase rate at $45 average
- Commemorative items: Higher margin, lower volume
- Post-race gear (finisher shirts sold separately): 8% rate

### Expo & Vendor Booths (5-8% of total)
- $2,500 per booth average
- Minimum 20 booths for small events
- Scale: 1 booth per 500 participants

### Media Rights (1-3% of total)
- Events >20,000 participants: $50,000 base × maturity factor
- Smaller events: $10,000 base × maturity factor
- Streaming rights increasingly valuable

## Revenue Benchmarks by Size

| Event Size | Revenue/Participant | Margin Target |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------|
| <10,000 | $180-220 | 10-15% |
| 10,000-25,000 | $220-280 | 15-22% |
| 25,000-50,000 | $280-350 | 20-28% |
| >50,000 | $350-450 | 25-35% |
